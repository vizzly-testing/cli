Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my pet takes more photos than my storage limit?

Don't worry! We'll send you a gentle notification (like a soft meow) when you're approaching your limit. You can always upgrade or we'll help you organize your photos better.

Can I change plans if I adopt more pets?

Absolutely! We love growing families. You can upgrade anytime, and we'll prorate the difference. Adding pets is always a good reason to celebrate!

Do you offer refunds if my pet doesn't like the service?

While we can't guarantee your pet's approval (cats can be particularly picky), we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our human customers.

Is there really unlimited storage for the Zoo Keeper plan?

Yes! We define "unlimited" as more storage than you could fill even if you took a photo of your pet every second for the next 100 years. Challenge accepted?
